# Macro Prudential Regulation
- Regulating **systemic risk**
	- [[Leverage Ratio]]
	- [[Bank Capital Buffers]]
- Focus on the entire financial system and interconnectivity of banks
- Minimize bank runs and moral hazard risk [[Information Economies]]
## 3 tools (financial safety net system)
- Deposit insurance
	- Intended to prevent bank runs
- Lender of last resort
	- Central bank provide emergency funding to banks with liquidity problems
		- See [[Economics MOC]] and [[Central Banks]]
	- Does this via the discount window
- Resolution laws
	- Special laws for bankruptcy
## Stress Testing
- Simulating adverse scenarios and seeing how balance sheet would react
- A measure of banks resilience to shocks
