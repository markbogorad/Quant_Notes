# Fisher Equation
$$\text{1 dollar for 1 yr} -> \frac{(1+r)}{(1+i)}=(1+\rho)$$
$$\text{Continously:} e^r=E^\rho*e^i$$
- Links one period inflation to one period spending power
- Logic:
	- Interest rates raise spending power
	- Inflation decreases spending power
