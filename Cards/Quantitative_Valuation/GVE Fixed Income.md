### GVE: Bond
$$V_0 = \frac{C}{r} + d_n(F-\frac{C}{r})$$
- Basically a flat annuity (the coupons) + a final payment (single period GVE)
### GVE: Floating Rate Note
$$rV_0 = (F-V_0) + r_0F$$
- A case of GVE done recursively
- This was also GVE with non constant rates
